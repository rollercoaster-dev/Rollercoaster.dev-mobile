The plan
0
Evaluate the flow
This map. Agree the spine, the role of each screen, where capture and overview live. We are here.
1
Lock the foundation
Commit one theme (Full Ride vs Still Water), the fonts, the shared primitives — one source of truth.
2
Screen by screen
In flow order. Each: audit → 2–3 variations → you pick → finalize. No moving on until done.
3
Stitch & walk
Walk the whole redesigned flow end-to-end. Check transitions, back behavior, consistency.
Calls to make before Phase 1
1. Theme — design in The Full Ride (neo-brutalist) or Still Water (autism-friendly, calm)? Or design once and prove it holds in both?
2. The overview — keep both the mini-timeline and the full Timeline screen, or collapse to one?
3. Evidence capture — 6 separate screens + FAB, or one unified capture flow?
4. Badge Designer up front — designing the badge right after naming a goal is a lot before any steps exist. Keep it there, make it skippable, or move it later?
Then Phase 2 starts with Goal list (entry), Focus Mode (hub), or wherever you feel the most pain.
